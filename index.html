About

Exploring latent spaces in language models

I'm currently engaged in research that delves into the hidden, high-dimensional latent spaces within language models. By analyzing these internal representations, I'm investigating how they encapsulate semantic nuances and syntactic structures, essentially reflecting a form of machine intuition. This exploration aims to quantify how well these latent features predict and relate to natural language patterns, similar to the intuitive leaps made by humans. The goal is to bridge the gap between abstract model representations and tangible linguistic understanding.
